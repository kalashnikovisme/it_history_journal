**FAQ Block:**
- **Q1:** Who was Charles Babbage?  
  **A1:** An English mathematician and inventor known as the "father of the computer" for his pioneering work on mechanical calculation devices.

- **Q2:** What is the Difference Engine?  
  **A2:** A mechanical calculator designed by Babbage to compute mathematical tables more accurately and efficiently.

- **Q3:** Why is the 1822 presentation significant?  
  **A3:** It marked one of the earliest formal proposals for a mechanical computer, laying foundational ideas for future programmable machines.

- **Q4:** Did Babbage complete the Difference Engine?  
  **A4:** No, it was never fully completed during his lifetime, but its concept influenced future developments in computing.
