**FAQ Block:**
Q1: Who was Charles Babbage?
A1: Charles Babbage was an English mathematician and inventor known as the "father of the computer" for his pioneering work on mechanical calculation devices.

Q2: What is the Difference Engine?
A2: The Difference Engine was an automatic mechanical calculator designed by Babbage to compute mathematical tables more accurately and efficiently.

Q3: Why is the 1822 presentation significant?
A3: It marked one of the earliest formal proposals for a mechanical computer, laying foundational ideas for future programmable machines.

Q4: Did Babbage complete the Difference Engine?
A4: No, Babbage's Difference Engine was never fully completed during his lifetime due to technical and financial challenges, but its concept influenced future computing.
