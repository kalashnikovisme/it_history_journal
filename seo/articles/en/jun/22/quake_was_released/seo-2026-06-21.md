**FAQ Block:**
- **Q:** Why is Quake considered a milestone in gaming history?  
  **A:** Because it introduced true 3D graphics, advanced multiplayer features, and fostered a vibrant modding community, influencing future games.

- **Q:** Who were the key developers behind Quake?  
  **A:** John Carmack, John Romero, Michael Abrash, and Tim Willits played pivotal roles in its development.

- **Q:** What technological innovations did Quake bring?  
  **A:** It used a true 3D engine, supported online multiplayer with QuakeWorld, and enabled demo recording for speedrunning.

- **Q:** How did Quake influence multiplayer gaming?  
  **A:** It popularized online multiplayer, introduced client-side prediction, and became a foundation for competitive gaming.

- **Q:** Is the Quake source code available?  
  **A:** Yes, id Software released it in 1999, allowing community modifications and ports. It’s available on GitHub.
