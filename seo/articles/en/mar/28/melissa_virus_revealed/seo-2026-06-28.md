Q2: How did the Melissa virus spread?  
A2: Through infected Word documents sent via email, exploiting macro features in Microsoft Word to propagate itself.  

Q3: What was the impact of the Melissa outbreak?  
A3: Massive email system failures, billions in damages, and significant changes in email security and macro controls.
