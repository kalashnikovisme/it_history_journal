Q3: How were domains registered in 1985?  
A3: Domains were registered manually via email requests to the NIC, with no automated systems or registrars.

Q4: Why was the .com domain created?  
A4: To serve as a top-level domain for commercial organizations, replacing the manual HOSTS.TXT system.

Q5: How many .com domains existed by the end of 1985?
