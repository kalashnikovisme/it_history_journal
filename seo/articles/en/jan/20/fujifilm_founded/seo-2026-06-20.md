Q2: What was the FUJIC computer?  
A2: The FUJIC was Japan’s first electronic computer, built by Fujifilm in 1956 for internal use.

Q3: How has Fujifilm contributed to data storage technology?  
A3: Fujifilm co-developed the LTO magnetic tape format, which remains vital for large-scale data storage today.

Q4: Did Fujifilm transition successfully after digital photography impacted film?

Q5: What is the significance of tape storage today?  
A5: Tape remains a reliable, cost-effective solution for cold data storage in cloud data centers.
