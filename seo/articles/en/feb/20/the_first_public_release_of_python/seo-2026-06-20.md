**FAQ Block:**

**Q1:** When was Python first released to the public?  
**A:** On February 20, 1991.

**Q2:** Who created Python?  
**A:** Guido van Rossum at the Centrum Wiskunde & Informatica (CWI) in Amsterdam.

**Q3:** Why is it called "Python"?  
**A:** Guido named it after Monty Python, a British comedy group, not the snake.

**Q4:** What were some features of Python's first release?  
**A:** Classes with inheritance, exception handling, functions, core data types, and modules.

**Q5:** How did Python's philosophy differ from other languages at the time?  
**A:** It prioritized readability and simplicity over complexity and formality.
