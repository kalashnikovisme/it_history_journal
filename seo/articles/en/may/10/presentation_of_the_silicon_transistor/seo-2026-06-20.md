Q1: Why was silicon chosen over germanium for transistors?  
A1: Silicon offers better heat resistance, lower current leakage, and greater stability, making it more suitable for microprocessors.  

Q2: Who developed the silicon transistor?  
A2: Gordon Teal, a Bell Labs engineer who later joined Texas Instruments, was instrumental in developing the silicon transistor.  

Q3: How did the silicon transistor impact technology?
