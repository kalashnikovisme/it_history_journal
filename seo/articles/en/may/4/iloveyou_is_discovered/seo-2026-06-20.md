**FAQ Block:**
- **Q:** What was the ILOVEYOU virus?  
  **A:** A computer worm that spread via email in 2000, causing widespread damage and financial loss.

- **Q:** How did the ILOVEYOU virus spread?  
  **A:** Through emails with a malicious attachment titled "LOVE-LETTER-FOR-YOU.TXT.vbs" that exploited Outlook's address book.

- **Q:** Who created the ILOVEYOU virus?  
  **A:** It was developed by Onel de Guzman, a student from the Philippines.

- **Q:** What were the consequences of the ILOVEYOU outbreak?  
  **A:** Billions in damages, changes in email security policies, and improved antivirus measures.
