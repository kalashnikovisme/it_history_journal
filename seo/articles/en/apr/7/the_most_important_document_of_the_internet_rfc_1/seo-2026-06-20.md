**FAQ Block:**
- *What is RFC 1?*  
  RFC 1 is the first Request for Comments document, published in 1969, that laid the groundwork for Internet communication protocols.
- *Why is RFC 1 considered important?*  
  It introduced the idea of open discussion and collaboration among developers, which became fundamental to the Internet's development.
- *Who authored RFC 1?*  
  Steve Crocker authored RFC 1 as a way to invite discussion on networking protocols.
- *How has RFC evolved over time?*  
  From a simple discussion forum, RFC now contains over 9,000 documents serving as standards and protocols for the Internet.
