**FAQ Block:**

Q1: What is the Osborne Effect?  
A1: It refers to a sales decline caused by announcing upcoming products too early, which discourages current sales.

Q2: Why did Osborne Computer fail?  
A2: After announcing new models prematurely, sales of their existing products plummeted, leading to bankruptcy.

Q3: How do companies avoid the Osborne Effect today?  
A3: They keep product details confidential until launch to prevent early leaks and customer hesitation.

Q4: What lessons can be learned from Osborne’s mistake?  
A4: Timing and secrecy in product announcements are crucial to maintaining sales and company stability.
