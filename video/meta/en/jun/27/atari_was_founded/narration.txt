June 27 in the History of Computer Science: The name Atari was officially registered in 1972. On this day, Nolan Bushnell and Ted Dabney took a leap from their modest beginnings to found one of gaming’s first giants. Their company’s first major hit, Pong, quickly filled arcades with eager players. Atari’s innovations not only shaped arcade culture but also brought video games into millions of homes, leaving a lasting legacy.
